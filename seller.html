Seller vs Builder

Qualities of a Seller

  • ✅ Talks about their(user) life, not your idea
  • ✅ Talks about specifics, not the hypotheticals around the problem user may be going through
    • ❌ We generally talk about hypotheticals
    • ❌ We talk about what our product could be
    • ❌ We ask questions like if we built this feature, would you be interested in paying for it
  • ✅ LISTEN! DON'T TALK
  • ✅ Ask great questions

Qualities of a Pseudo Builder

  • ❌ Thinks of an great idea
  • ❌ Focuses on hypotheticals
  • ❌ Starts building without even talking to single user
  • ❌ Keeps building until the product is perfect
  • ❌ Is always scared of talking to users, so keeps adding features, to escape the reality
  • ❌ Thinks of scaling
  • ❌ Keeps building for months and sometimes years
  • ❌ Never sells. Product dies.

Qualities of a Real Builder

  • ✅ Builds an mvp after talking to the user refer seller
  • ✅ Never thinks of scaling at the first place
  • ✅ Not afraid to ship imperfect or "ugly" MVP to get things moving. This works as long as the product is useful enough that customers don’t mind its flaws.
  • ✅ Now a days with ai, we can build less imperfect and less ugly looking MVP
  • ✅ Keeps talking to user, keeps iterating until you make 100 users love your product

    • ❗️ Note: Please ignore outliers and focus on the general idea. We are not trying to create a perfect science here. We are just trying to create a general understanding of what it takes to sell and build.

    💡 Every team needs a combination of a seller and a builder
